SuperPlane ships with a set of built-in templates you can copy into your workspace and adapt.
This page gives you a quick tour of what each template is for, plus screenshots you can reference as you build.

<Aside>
  Use the gallery view to scan the shape of the workflow, then click
  the image to view the full canvas.
</Aside>
